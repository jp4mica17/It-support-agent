# New Hire IT Onboarding Guide

## Welcome
This guide covers everything you need to get set up on day one. Complete these steps in order to avoid access issues during your first week.

## Day 1 Checklist

### 1. Receive and power on your device
IT will have your laptop ready at your desk or shipped to your home address. Power it on and complete the Windows/macOS setup wizard using your corporate email when prompted.

### 2. Set your initial password
You will receive a temporary password via email to your personal address before your start date. Go to https://aka.ms/sspr to set your permanent password immediately.

### 3. Set up Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)
MFA is required before you can access any corporate system. Go to https://aka.ms/mfasetup on your device and follow the MFA Setup guide. Register at least two methods.

### 4. Enroll your device in MDM (if not auto-enrolled)
- **Windows**: Go to Settings → Accounts → Access work or school → Connect
- **Mac**: Your device should auto-enroll — open Company Portal to verify compliance

### 5. Connect to corporate Wi-Fi
Network name: **Corp-Secure** (requires device to be Intune/Jamf compliant)
Credentials will sync automatically via MDM policy once enrolled.

### 6. Install required software
Open the **Company Portal** app (Windows) or **Jamf Self Service** (Mac) to install:
- Microsoft 365 (Outlook, Teams, Word, Excel)
- Cisco AnyConnect (VPN)
- Slack
- CrowdStrike Falcon (endpoint protection — installs automatically)

### 7. Access your email
Open Outlook and sign in with your corporate email. Your email, calendar, and contacts will sync within a few minutes.

### 8. Join your team in Slack and Microsoft Teams
Check your onboarding email for your team's primary communication channel.

## Your First Week — What to Expect
IT hosts a 30-minute onboarding session every other week covering systems, security policies, and how to get help. Your manager will send you the calendar invite. Attendance is strongly encouraged.
